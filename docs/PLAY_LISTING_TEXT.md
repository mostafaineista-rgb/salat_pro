## Full description (English)

Salat Pro is a calm, premium prayer companion built for clarity and reliability.

**Key features**
- Accurate daily prayer times based on your location or a chosen city
- Qibla direction with a clean, easy-to-read interface
- Hijri date and Islamic calendar
- Adhan notifications at prayer times (with sound options)
- Nearby mosques (optional, when you use the feature)
- Optional weather snapshot for your area

**Privacy-first**
- No in-app ads
- No selling of personal data
- Permissions are requested only when needed for app features (e.g., location for local prayer times and nearby mosques)

Support: support@furatbyte.com
