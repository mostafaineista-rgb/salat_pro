Salat Pro — Privacy Policy

Effective date: · Publisher: Furatbyte Studio

Summary

Salat Pro is designed to work primarily on-device. We do not sell personal data and we do not run in-app ads. Some features make network requests to public services to provide results (e.g., nearby mosques, weather, city search).

Data we use

  • Location (optional): used to compute prayer times, Qibla direction, find nearby mosques, and fetch optional weather.
  • Notifications: prayer alerts are scheduled locally on your device when you enable them.
  • Local storage: your preferences may be saved locally (e.g., calculation method, selected sound) to improve the experience.

Third-party services

When you use certain features, the app may send requests to public services. Depending on the feature, requests may include your search text or location coordinates.

  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim: city/address search (query text).
  • Overpass API (OpenStreetMap): nearby mosques (location coordinates in the query).
  • Open‑Meteo: optional weather (location coordinates).
  • Hadith source via CDN/GitHub: downloads content for hadith features.

Advertising, selling, and tracking

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not show in-app ads.
  • We do not use advertising ID or cross-app tracking SDKs.
