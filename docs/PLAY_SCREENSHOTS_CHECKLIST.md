# Play Store screenshots & graphics checklist (Salat Pro)

## Required (minimum)

- Phone screenshots: **at least 2**, recommended **6–8**

## Recommended phone screenshots (6–8)

1) **Home / Today**
   - Shows today’s prayer times clearly (and next prayer highlight if present).

2) **Qibla**
   - Clear compass / direction UI.

3) **Notifications / Adhan alert**
   - Settings section showing adhan alert enabled and sound selection, or a scheduled/test alert screen.

4) **Calendar / Hijri**
   - Hijri date and calendar view.

5) **Nearby mosques**
   - List/map result view (ensure it doesn’t show personal addresses; just general results).

6) **Settings**
   - Key toggles and language (Arabic/English) if available.

Optional extras (if you have good screens)
- **Adhan sound picker**
- **Azkar / daily reminder / hadith moment** (if it’s a core feature)
- **Privacy policy / About** (helps reviewers/users trust the app)

## Feature graphic & icon

- **App icon**: high-res, clean edges
- **Feature graphic**: match your app brand colors; avoid excessive text

## Review-safe tips (avoid rejections)

- Don’t imply medical/health claims.
- Don’t show “install APK” / “download APK” wording anywhere.
- If a screenshot includes location, avoid showing a precise home address.
